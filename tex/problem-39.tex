\begin{pro_no_count}[Nguyen Van Huyen]
Let $a, \,b, \,c, \,d$ be positive real numbers. Prove that
$$\frac{a}{b}+\frac{b}{c}+\frac{c}{d}+\frac{d}{a} \geqslant 4 + \frac{(a-b)^2}{ab+bc+cd+da}.$$
\end{pro_no_count}
